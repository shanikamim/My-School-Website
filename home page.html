JOYDEBPUR GOVT. GIRLS' HIGH SCHOOL

WELCOME



ABOUT US: JOydebpur Govt. Girls' High School is an academic institute code(EIIN) is 108954. It was established in 1905. It is situated center of Gazipur City and near Bhawal Rajbari. The school was named after queen "Rani Bilashmoni". It has passed more than 100 years. The education level started from 6th Grade up to Secondary School Certificate exam (S.S.C.). The school has an information exchange program with John Marshall Middle School in Wichita, Kansas.



CAMPUS LIFE: Our campus becomes live from 6 am in the morning at the heart of gazipur. It is just beside the historical place Rajbari maidan.The morning assembly with the fresh Girls’ offering an eye catching views to the passerby. With the help of the Government the building is going upward giving spacious classroom facilities. Due to acute shortage of infrastructural facilities our head master has to sit in another building out side the campus. School is an institution well known for its educational environment,well- planned syllabus, emphasis on discipline and qualified teachers, organized and efficient management, consistency in Junior Scholarship and SSC results,co-curricular activities, active participation and quality performances of its students in each and every competition either in academic or co-curricular fields



MISSION: Our mission is to develop young women with active and creative minds, a sense of understanding and compassion for others and the courage to act on their beliefs. We the teachers try to develop each child's spiritual, moral, intellectual, social, emotional and physical development. Again we want our students to develop them like a self dependent, adult. We are offering a diverse education in a safe, supportive environment that promotes self-discipline, motivation and excellence in learning.



VISION: It has become the most preferred educational institution In the Gazipur district as well as in Bangladesh. By the introduction of innovative and modern curriculum and with the help of the creative and dedicated staff. we are offering and excellent education for the students.
